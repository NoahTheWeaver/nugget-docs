# How to Give Feedback on These Docs

We'll use Hypothesis to collect feedback since it makes it super easy for you to nitpick my writing in detail, which is the whole goal here.

## Setup (about 2 minutes)

1. Go to [https://hypothes.is/signup](https://hypothes.is/signup).
2. Click **Sign up with Google** and go through the signup process.
3. Install the [Hypothesis Chrome extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/hypothesis-web-pdf-annota/bjfhmglciegochdpefhhlphglcehbmek).

Once that's done, you can highlight any text at [wyatt-docs.nuggetscientific.com](https://wyatt-docs.nuggetscientific.com) and share your feedback. I'll be notified. This is way more efficient than endless emails or Google Chats.

## What to Look Out For

A few things to flag as you read:

- Anything that makes you scrunch up your nose or that you had to read more than once to understand. Seriously, if you go, "Oh wait... oh no, I get it now," include that as a comment.
- Anything that seems flat out wrong (obviously).
- Any follow-up questions this triggered.

Shoot me a message in Google Chat if you have questions.
