# Accounting & Finance

Guides for the accounting team and leadership.

- [Posting Timesheets to GL](./timesheet-posting) — Monthly process for posting direct labor costs
- [Task-Level Cost Tracking](./task-analytics) — How analytic accounts allocate costs to projects
- [Per Diem Administration](./per-diem) — Reviewing and paying per diems

::: danger Payroll and Labor Cost Allocation
Payroll is **not** run through Odoo. Payroll runs through QuickBooks (or a future payroll provider). After payroll runs, a portion of those costs is journaled into Odoo as Direct Labor.

The **Timesheet Posting** module allocates direct labor costs to specific projects and tasks via analytic accounts. It is the bridge between "hours worked" and "cost allocated." It does **not** handle overhead labor — only time logged against customer projects flows through this module.
:::
