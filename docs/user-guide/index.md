# User Guide

Pick the guide that matches your role. If you wear multiple hats, you'll find what you need across sections.

## [Field Service](/user-guide/field-service/)
For field service engineers and dispatch coordinators. Covers service contracts, scheduling, timesheets, per diem, and service request workflows.

## [Operations](/user-guide/operations/)
For warehouse staff, salespeople, and operations managers. Covers inventory, purchasing, sales orders, shipping, receiving, and BOMs.

## [Accounting & Finance](/user-guide/accounting/)
For the accounting team and leadership. Covers the chart of accounts, AP/AR, financial close, per diem billing, and management reporting.
