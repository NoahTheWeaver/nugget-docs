# Why Wyatt

## What Is This?

Wyatt is the codename for Nugget Scientific's ERP system, built on Odoo 19 Enterprise. It replaces a patchwork of QuickBooks, a custom-built CRM, and Google Sheets with a single platform that handles accounting, inventory, field service, sales, and purchasing.

## Why Are We Doing This?

Three problems drove this project:

1. **We couldn't see our financials clearly.** No gross profit by customer, project, or business unit. Leadership was making decisions without the numbers to back them up.

2. **Operations ran on tribal knowledge.** No visibility into sub-component inventory, no resource scheduling data, manual SLA tracking. When someone was out, their work was a black box.

3. **Too many manual handoffs.** Slow receiving, no status-differentiated inventory, copy-paste between systems. People spent time moving data instead of doing their jobs.

Wyatt fixes this by putting everything in one place. When a system ships, accounting knows. When a part is consumed, inventory updates. When a field engineer logs time, per diems calculate automatically.

## Why Odoo?

- **Open source** — We own our data and can modify or leave the platform whenever we want
- **Cost** — Over 100% cheaper than most ERPs, 25% cheaper than the nearest competitor
- **Simplicity** — Easy to learn, no enterprise jargon
- **Proven** — Teams we trust launched on Odoo with the same implementation group and built a $26M business
