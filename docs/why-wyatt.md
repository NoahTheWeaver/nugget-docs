# Why Wyatt

## What Is This?

Wyatt is the codename for Nugget Scientific's ERP[^erp] system, built on Odoo 19 Enterprise. It replaces a patchwork of QuickBooks, a custom-built CRM, and Google Sheets with a single platform that handles accounting, inventory, field service, sales, and purchasing.

[^erp]: Enterprise resource planning. The one tool that runs the back of the house. Don't worry, we'll never use this term again.

## Why Are We Doing This?

Three problems drove this project:

1. **Everything lived in Google Chat and email.** Tasks, handoffs, status updates scattered across threads. When someone was out, their work was a black box.

2. **Operations ran on tribal knowledge.** No visibility into sub-component inventory, no resource scheduling data, manual SLA tracking. People spent time moving data between systems instead of doing their jobs.

3. **Financials were fuzzy.** No gross profit by customer, project, or business unit. Decisions got made on gut instead of data.

Wyatt fixes this by putting everything in one place. When a system ships, accounting knows. When a part is consumed, inventory updates. When a field engineer logs time, per diems calculate automatically.

## Why Odoo?

- **Open source:** we own our data and can modify or leave the platform whenever we want.
- **Simplicity:** easy to learn, no enterprise jargon.
- **Proven:** teams we trust launched on Odoo with the same implementation group and built a $26M business.
